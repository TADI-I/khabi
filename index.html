Company Overview

Who We Are

Khabi Engineering Pty Ltd is a 100% Black-owned South African company established in 2013 and based in Middelburg, Mpumalanga. We deliver integrated Engineering, Construction, Manufacturing, Cleaning Services, and supply of Industrial Equipment to public and private sector clients across South Africa.

We are committed not only to delivering quality services but also to playing a transformative role in society — addressing unemployment, skills shortages, poverty, and social challenges.

Our Vision

To be a forefront Engineering & Construction industry and cleaning service provider, recognised for excellence, professionalism, and meaningful contribution to skills transfer and socio-economic growth.

Our Mission

  • Deliver quality services right the first time
  • Empower communities through skills development
  • Build a competitive, sustainable company
  • Contribute positively to the corporate and social environment

Policy Statement

SHE is a Core Value.

Khabi Engineering Pty Ltd integrates Safety, Health and Environmental principles into every activity we undertake. We recognise the importance of protecting the health and safety of our employees, clients, environment, and contractors — and commit to turning SHE challenges into opportunities.

We use innovative process design and technology to create safer, cleaner, and more sustainable work environments — caring for all employees, clients, contractors, and the communities in which we operate.

Issued: January 2024  |  Rev: 00  |  Next Review: January 2027

Safety

Zero accidents, zero injuries. Every person on our sites goes home safe.

Health

Protecting workers from occupational illness through training and safe systems.

Environment

Proper housekeeping, clean facilities, and environmentally responsible practices.

Our Standards

What We Stand By

01

02

How We Deliver

Our Commitments

Risk Management

Implement systems to identify, assess, monitor, and control hazards on every project.

Legal Compliance

Meet and exceed all applicable environmental, health, fire, and safety laws and regulations.

Sustainable Development

Promote sustainable development by integrating SHE in all relevant decision-making processes.

Safe Systems of Work

Design, implement, provide, and enforce safe systems of work on all projects.

Hazard Planning

Plan ahead by reviewing hazards and complying with relevant client plant safety procedures on all field visits.

Training & Behaviour

Encourage safe work behaviours and train all employees and managers in general safety practices.

Worker Participation

Involve employees at all levels in the SHE process, with commitment to consultation and participation of workers and their representatives.

PPE Provision

Provide all employees and contractors with all relevant, required, compliant PPE for all specific activities to be executed.

Continual Improvement

Sustain continual improvement by annually reviewing our policy, standards, objectives, and programmes with the intent of minimising SHE risks.
