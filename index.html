Company Overview

Who We Are

Khabi Engineering Pty Ltd is a 100% Black-owned South African company established in 2013 and based in Middelburg, Mpumalanga. We deliver integrated Engineering, Construction, Manufacturing, Cleaning Services, and supply of Industrial Equipment to public and private sector clients across South Africa.

We are committed not only to delivering quality services but also to playing a transformative role in society — addressing unemployment, skills shortages, poverty, and social challenges.

Our Vision

To be a forefront Engineering & Construction industry and cleaning service provider, recognised for excellence, professionalism, and meaningful contribution to skills transfer and socio-economic growth.

Our Mission

  • Deliver quality services right the first time
  • Empower communities through skills development
  • Build a competitive, sustainable company
  • Contribute positively to the corporate and social environment

01

Building & Civil Construction

We deliver high-quality building and civil construction services for government housing projects, private developments, and infrastructure. With a proven track record including RDP housing projects across the Eastern Cape, KwaZulu-Natal, and Mpumalanga, our team has the experience to handle complex, multi-year contracts.

  • Low-cost housing (RDP) construction
  • Double storey residential builds
  • Infrastructure and civil works
  • Site establishment
02

Renovations

Full renovation services for commercial, industrial, and residential properties. From plastering and painting to structural modifications — we restore, upgrade, and transform existing structures.

  • Commercial & industrial renovations
  • Plastering and painting
  • School and institutional facilities
  • Office park upgrades
03

Cleaning Services

Our cleaning division has 7+ years of operation serving 15 private clients on a recurring basis. We specialise in industrial plant cleaning with deep exposure to Samancor facilities including M1–M4, Crusher plant, PSP, Bunkers, and Workshops.

  • Industrial plant cleaning (Samancor-experienced)
  • Site establishment & cleaning
  • Housekeeping services
  • Regular maintenance cleaning for private clients
04

Manufacturing

Bricks manufacturing and structural steel and aluminium fabrication to support construction projects and supply clients directly.

  • Bricks manufacturing
  • Steel works & fabrication
  • Aluminium works
05

Equipment & Machinery Supply

We source and supply industrial equipment, furniture, and yellow plant machinery to construction and industrial clients on a contract basis.

  • Industrial equipment supply
  • Furniture sourcing & delivery
  • Yellow plant & construction machinery
